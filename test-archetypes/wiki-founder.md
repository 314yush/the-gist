# My Knowledge Wiki - Startup Founder

## Startup Fundamentals
- **Product-market fit** - solving a real problem people pay for
- **MVP** - minimum viable product, build-measure-learn
- **Moats** - network effects, switching costs, brand, scale
- **Business models** - SaaS, marketplace, transactional

## Fundraising
- Pre-seed, Seed, Series A/B/C stages
- SAFE notes vs priced rounds
- Cap tables and dilution
- Term sheets: liquidation preferences, anti-dilution

## Team Building
- Hiring for 0→1 vs 1→10 stages
- Equity compensation and vesting schedules
- Culture and values documentation
- Remote vs hybrid team dynamics

## Financial Metrics
- MRR/ARR (Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue)
- Burn rate and runway
- Unit economics and contribution margin
- Rule of 40 (growth rate + profit margin)

- Vertical SaaS vs horizontal plays
