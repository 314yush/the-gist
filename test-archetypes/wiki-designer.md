# My Knowledge Wiki - Designer

## Design Fundamentals
- **Visual hierarchy** - guiding the eye through size, color, contrast
- **Gestalt principles** - proximity, similarity, closure, continuity
- **Grid systems** - 8pt grid, 12-column layouts, modular design
- **Typography** - kerning, leading, tracking, font pairing

## Color Theory
- Color wheel relationships: complementary, analogous, triadic
- HSL vs RGB for digital design
- Accessibility: WCAG AA contrast ratios (4.5:1 for text)

## Tools I Use
- Figma for UI/UX design and prototyping
- After Effects for motion design
- Principle for micro-interactions
- Blender for 3D elements

## Design Systems
- Atomic design methodology (atoms → molecules → organisms)
- Design tokens for consistency
- Component libraries and variants

## Current Interests
- Neumorphism and glassmorphism trends
- Variable fonts and fluid typography
- Spatial design for AR/VR interfaces
